Verena Müller (she/her) is a PhD Candidate at the School of Social Sciences and Technology at Technical University of Munich. After completing her degree in Law, she joined the Professorship for Law, Innovation and Legal Design at TU Munich as a researcher and doctoral candidate. Her research interests lie in the intersection of technology, society and regulation. Her current project focuses on AI Regulation and environmental sustainability, thereby exploring innovative approaches to integrate law and AI practises to promote “Sustainability by Design”. During Fall and Winter Term 2025/26, Verena is an International Visiting Graduate Student at the Just Sustainability Design Lab at University of Toronto.
